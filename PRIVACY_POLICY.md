However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

You may withdraw consent to collect, use, or disclose your personal data at any time, but this may affect our ability to serve you.

You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
